What is Vestal?

Vestal is the ultimate platform for finding roommates or shared homes. Connect tenants and spaces quickly, securely, and with real compatibility.

Values & Goals

  • Transparency: All information validated and accessible.
  • Security: Chats and data protected by modern protocols.
  • Compatibility: Smart matching for harmonious living.
  • Community: We foster respect and collaboration.

Privacy & Data Handling

At Vestal we value your privacy. We collect only the data necessary for the platform’s operation and never share it with third parties without your express consent. For more details, see our Privacy Policy.
