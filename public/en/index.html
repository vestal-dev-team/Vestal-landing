What is Vestal?

Vestal is the ultimate platform for finding roommates and shared homes quickly and securely. Using our unique ID system, users can browse registered listings and submit applications in just a few clicks.

Once you apply, current tenants review your profile and compatibility score—powered by our smart matching algorithm. If approved, a private chat opens where you can get to know each other, exchange documents, and schedule an in-person viewing via our integrated calendar.

Values & Goals

  • Transparency: All listings and user data are verified and kept up to date.
  • Security: Encrypted chats, secure authentication, and robust data protection.
  • Compatibility: A matching algorithm that considers lifestyle, budget, and preferences.
  • Community: We foster respect, collaboration, and wellbeing for all members.
  • Innovation: Continuously improving technology to deliver the best experience.

Privacy & Data Handling

At Vestal we take your privacy seriously and protect your data with state-of-the-art protocols. We collect only the data necessary to run the platform and never share it without your consent.

For full details on how we handle user records, chats, location data, and authentication, see our Privacy Policy.
